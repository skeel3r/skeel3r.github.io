About Me

Bio

I've grown up dreaming about a world only seen in books. Ever since I can remember I would build things, imagine new ideas, and try to figure out how things work. My unique educational experience in a Spanish immersion program, at the International High School, and B.A. in International Studies has given me a broad understanding of the world we live in. It's driven me to want to change the world.

I decided to achieve this by pursuing Robotics and focusing my passion towards creating solutions to problems that can help improve our lives and our world. I enjoy doing projects, and often have a couple on going. I love hearing about new technology and advancements in research so I can think of what new things it can be applied to.

I like to think of myself as easy going and personable. I've unplugged myself from social media to really focus on the people around me. I enjoy nothing more than talking with a friend and having a beer playing golf or frolf! I equally enjoy discussing last nights football game as I do debating the nuances of autoencoders for convolutional neural networks in deep learning.

If you've read this far then you've probably got a good glimpse into who I am. If you have questions about my work, want to offer me a job, or just say hi, please send me an email. I love meeting new people, so send me a message!
